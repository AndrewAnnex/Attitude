attitude.stereonet.plane_errors(axes, covariance_matrix, sheet='upper', **kwargs)[source]¶

kwargs: traditional_layout boolean [True]

Lay the stereonet out traditionally, with north at the pole of the diagram. The default is a more natural and intuitive visualization with vertical at the pole and the compass points of strike around the equator. Thus, longitude at the equator represents strike and latitude represents apparent dip at that azimuth.
